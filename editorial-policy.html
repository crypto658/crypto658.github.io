Editorial Policy

Crypto Insights SA is an informational and editorial website focused on helping South African readers understand cryptocurrency topics more clearly. This page explains how content is selected, written, reviewed, and updated.

What we aim to publish

We focus on practical, South Africa-relevant topics such as Bitcoin and Ethereum basics, BTC/ZAR market context, exchange comparisons, security, wallet use, tax basics, and general educational explainers. We try to prioritise clarity, usefulness, and local relevance over hype, sensationalism, or prediction-driven content.

How topics are chosen

Topics are selected based on common questions South African readers tend to have, including buying crypto with rand, comparing platforms, improving wallet safety, understanding tax basics, and interpreting market moves in local context.

How content is reviewed

Articles are reviewed for readability, consistency, and basic factual coherence before publication. Because exchange features, regulation, tax treatment, fees, and market conditions can change, readers should still verify important time-sensitive details with the original provider or an appropriately qualified professional.

Financial, legal, and tax limitations

Crypto Insights SA does not provide personalised financial advice, regulated investment advice, legal advice, or tax advice. Content is published for general educational and editorial purposes only. Readers remain responsible for their own decisions.

Corrections and updates

If a reader spots a factual issue, outdated point, broken link, or unclear explanation, they can report it by email at info@cryptoinsights.co.za. We aim to review correction requests and update pages when appropriate.

Commercial independence

This site is not an exchange, broker, wallet provider, or investment platform. The purpose of the site is informational publishing. Where advertising is shown, that does not change the site's core editorial aim of publishing practical educational content.

Reader responsibility

Crypto markets are volatile and risky. Readers should treat the site as a starting point for learning, not as a substitute for personal advice, legal guidance, or formal due diligence.
