Contact Us

We'd love to hear from you. Whether you have a trading question, a collaboration idea, or just want to say hello — our inbox is open.

📬 Get In Touch

The fastest way to reach us is by email at info@cryptoinsights.co.za. We monitor this address daily and aim to respond to all enquiries within two (2) business days. For urgent matters, please mark your subject line with "URGENT".

We are based in Johannesburg, South Africa, and operate across all South African time zones.

🤝 How We Can Help

We handle a broad range of topics. Here's a quick breakdown of what we respond to and what works best:

  • Trading Questions & Analysis — If you're trying to understand a signal, need help interpreting market data, or want to discuss a specific coin or strategy, send us a message. While we can't give personalised financial advice, we can point you toward relevant resources on the Site.
  • Content Partnerships — If you're a crypto project, blockchain developer, or financial educator interested in contributing articles, co-branding content, or cross-promoting on our platform, we'd like to hear your proposal. Please include details about your project, audience size, and what you're looking for.
  • Media & Press Enquiries — If you're a journalist, blogger, or researcher covering cryptocurrency in South Africa, we're happy to provide data, quotes, or background information. Contact us at least five (5) business days before your publication deadline.
  • Bug Reports & Technical Issues — Spotted something broken on the Site? Incorrect price data? Let us know and we'll look into it promptly.
  • General Feedback — Ideas for new articles, features you'd like to see, or just want to tell us what we can do better? We read every message and use your feedback to shape what we do next.

⚠️ What We Can't Help With

To be transparent: we cannot provide personalised investment advice, recommend specific buy/sell positions, or manage third-party funds. If you need structured financial guidance, please consult a registered financial services provider (FSP) in South Africa. Similarly, we cannot assist with disputes related to external exchanges, wallets, or DeFi protocols — please contact those services directly.
