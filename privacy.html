Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

Introduction

Crypto Insights SA ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you visit our website at https://crypto658.github.io.

Information We Collect Automatically

Device and Usage Data

We do not collect personal identification data (like names or emails) directly. However, our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) and third-party services automatically collect:

  • IP addresses
  • Browser type
  • Operating system
  • Referral URLs
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Date and time of visits

This information is used for analytics and to improve user experience. It cannot be used to identify you personally (anonymized).

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience. Here's what they do:

  • Essential cookies — Enable basic site functionality
  • Analytics cookies — Help us understand how visitors use the site
  • Google AdSense cookies — Used by Google to serve ads and personalize them based on your browsing behavior

Google AdSense uses the DART cookie to show ads based on your previous visits to our site and other sites on the internet. You can opt out of DART cookies at: Google's ads & privacy policy.

Third-Party Services

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display ads. Google uses your browsing history to show personalized ads. Google is a third-party ad provider and uses cookies for its own purposes. Google's use of DoubleClick cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits to this site and/or other sites on the internet.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting YourOnlineChoices.com.

GitHub Pages

Our site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect standard server logs. Refer to GitHub's privacy statement for details.

CoinGecko API

Our site fetches cryptocurrency price data from CoinGecko's public API. No user data is sent to CoinGecko — the prices are the same for everyone and do not identify individual users.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Your personal identification (name, email, phone, address)
  • Your crypto wallet addresses or private keys
  • Your Luno or exchange account information
  • Payment or financial information

We do not sell or share your personal data. We do not track individual users for identification purposes.

Children's Privacy

Crypto Insights SA is not targeted at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

Under South African law (POPIA), you have the right to:

  • Know what personal data we hold
  • Request correction of inaccuracies
  • Request deletion of any data we hold

Contact us at: privacy@cryptoinsights.co.za

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Check back for updates. We will notify users by updating the date at the top of this page.
