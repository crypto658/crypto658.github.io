Luno Trading Strategies: Momentum & Volume

March 30, 2025

Last updated: Loading...

Trading on Luno South Africa doesn't require expensive tools or complex indicators. By understanding momentum and volume, you can identify breakout opportunities and avoid fakeouts. Here's our proven strategy.

The Core Idea

Momentum trading means buying a coin when it's already going up, and selling before the trend reverses. Volume is your confirmation — a real breakout has increasing volume, while a fakeout shows declining volume.

Step 1: Spot Volume Spikes

A volume spike is when trading volume suddenly jumps 1.5x - 2x above its 24-hour average. On Luno, this means more people are buying/selling than usual.

  • Compare: current hour volume vs. average of previous 20 hours
  • Signal: volume spike + price breaking above recent resistance = BUY
  • Warning: volume spike + price stuck = potential trap, AVOID

Step 2: Identify Support & Resistance

Look at the last 24-48 hours of price action on Luno's chart:

  • Resistance: the highest price the coin has struggled to break through
  • Support: the lowest price where buyers have stepped in
  • Breakout: when price breaks above resistance with strong volume

Real Example: TRX Pump

In recent weeks, TRX/ZAR on Luno showed all three bullish signals:

  1. Volume spike: 2.5x average volume in a 4-hour window
  2. Resistance break: crossed R2.50 after hovering below it for 3 days
  3. Positive sentiment: social media buzz increased around TRX

Traders who entered at R2.55 exited at R3.20 — a 25% gain in under 48 hours.

Step 3: Set Your Exit

Never enter a trade without knowing when to exit:

  • Take profit: set a target when you buy (e.g., 5-10% gain)
  • Stop-loss: if the price falls 3% below your entry, sell immediately
  • Trail your stop: if the price keeps going up, move your stop-loss up too

Momentum Continuation (Pro Tip)

Don't sell early if momentum is still strong! Instead of selling at a fixed +5%, watch for:

  • Volume still elevated or increasing ✓
  • Price trending higher each hour ✓
  • Positive news/sentiment about the coin ✓

When momentum fades (volume drops, price stalls, news turns negative), that's your signal to exit — not an arbitrary percentage.

Common Mistakes

  • ❌ Buying after a 20% pump — you're too late, exit is imminent
  • ❌ Ignoring volume — a breakout on low volume is usually a trap
  • ❌ Holding through a reversal — "it'll bounce back" rarely works in crypto
  • ❌ Trading without a plan — know your entry, target, and stop BEFORE buying

Summary

  1. Watch for volume spikes on Luno's ZAR pairs
  2. Confirm with resistance breakout
  3. Check sentiment (news, social media)
  4. Enter with a limit order (not market)
  5. Set a stop-loss and take-profit
  6. Exit when momentum fades, not too early
