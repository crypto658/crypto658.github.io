Top Altcoins for South African Investors (2025)

March 28, 2025

Last updated: Loading...

Not all altcoins are created equal. For South African investors trading on Luno, we prioritize established tokens with strong liquidity (R1M+ daily volume), active communities, and momentum-driven trading signals. Here are our top picks.

🏆 Our Criteria

  • Liquidity: Minimum R1 million daily volume on Luno
  • Momentum: Volume > 20h average, price breaking resistance
  • Fundamentals: Active development, real use case, strong community
  • Sentiment: Positive social media & news coverage

1. Ethereum (ETH/ZAR)

The king of altcoins. ETH remains the backbone of DeFi, NFTs, and Layer 2 scaling. With the recent Shanghai upgrade and growing DApp ecosystem, ETH continues to lead.

  • Liquidity: R50M+ daily
  • Risk: Low to Medium
  • Strategy: Hold + trade dips

2. Solana (SOL/ZAR)

High-speed, low fees. Solana's fast transaction times and growing DeFi ecosystem make it a strong contender. The recent memecoin surge boosted volume and community interest.

  • Liquidity: R15M+ daily
  • Risk: Medium
  • Strategy: Momentum trades + hold through strong cycles

3. XRP (XRP/ZAR)

Better than expected resilience. Despite ongoing regulatory uncertainty, XRP maintains strong volume and is one of the most actively traded coins on Luno. The SEC case resolution could be a catalyst.

  • Liquidity: R20M+ daily
  • Risk: Medium (news-driven spikes)
  • Strategy: Swing trades on momentum signals

4. TRON (TRX/ZAR)

Steady climber. TRX has consistently pumped on Luno with strong volume spikes. Low price point makes it accessible for small account traders.

  • Liquidity: R5M+ daily
  • Risk: Medium to High
  • Strategy: Momentum trades with tight stop-losses

5. Arbitrum (ARB/ZAR)

Ethereum Layer 2 leader. Arbitrum processes transactions for popular DApps with Ethereum-like security but lower fees. Growing TVL and user adoption.

  • Liquidity: R3M+ daily
  • Risk: Medium
  • Strategy: Buy on dips, hold for medium-term

How to Trade These on Luno

For each coin, apply the momentum + volume strategy:

  1. Wait for a volume spike (1.5x average or higher)
  2. Confirm resistance break on the chart
  3. Check news sentiment — is there positive catalysts?
  4. Enter with a limit buy order
  5. Set stop-loss at 3% below entry
  6. Sell when momentum fades, not at a fixed percentage

Risk Warning

Altcoins can be volatile. Never invest more than you're willing to lose. Always use stop-losses. Past performance doesn't guarantee future results. This is not financial advice.
