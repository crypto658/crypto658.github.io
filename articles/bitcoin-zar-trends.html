Bitcoin ZAR: Key Trends to Watch This Month

April 1, 2025

Bitcoin continues to trade in a tight range against the South African Rand, reflecting global consolidation and local currency volatility. Here's what we're watching this month.

Support & Resistance

Major Support: R1,980,000 ZAR

First Resistance: R2,250,000 ZAR

Major Resistance: R2,400,000 ZAR

Break through R2.25M could open the door for a run toward R2.4M. Conversely, a fall below R1.98M might see BTC test R1.8M in a broader market correction.

Technical Indicators

The daily chart is showing a potential descending wedge pattern with the RSI hovering near oversold at 35-38. Volume has been declining, signaling decreased retail participation — but historically, declining volume often precedes sharp moves.

Macroeconomic Context

The rand has weakened slightly against the dollar (USD/ZAR ~18.2), which has put upward pressure on BTC/ZAR even as global BTC/USD remains range-bound. Key local factors include:

  • Fed rate decisions — Higher US rates attract capital to USD-denominated assets, putting downward pressure on emerging market currencies like ZAR
  • Load shedding improvements — Reduced energy costs are strengthening local market sentiment slightly
  • SARB policy — Tightening local monetary policy may strengthen the rand, temporarily reducing BTC/ZAR valuations

Trading Strategies on Luno

For Luno traders, the current environment suggests a momentum-based approach: enter long when BTC breaks R2.25M with strong volume, and set a trailing stop-loss at 3% to protect against sudden reversals.
