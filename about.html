About Crypto Insights SA

Our Mission

Crypto Insights SA exists to give South African cryptocurrency investors actionable, data-driven insights that help them make informed decisions. We believe that with the right tools and information, everyday investors can trade smarter — not harder.

What We Do

We run a combination of:

  • Market Analysis — In-depth articles on Bitcoin, Ethereum, and altcoins, focused on ZAR trading pairs
  • Trading Signals — Momentum-based signals using volume analysis, sentiment tracking, and AI scoring
  • Educational Content — Guides on trading strategies (momentum, breakout trading, DeFi), risk management, and crypto tax law for South Africans
  • Live Crypto Ticker — Real-time ZAR prices tracked directly on this site

Who We Are

We're a small team of South African developers and crypto enthusiasts passionate about making crypto trading accessible and understandable. Our goal is to empower everyday investors with the same tools and strategies that professional traders use.

Our Bot

We've built a momentum-based automated trading bot that scans the Luno exchange 24/7, looking for:

  • Volume spikes (1.5× average)
  • Resistance breakouts
  • Positive news and social media sentiment
  • AI-scored confidence > 0.6

The bot executes trades automatically, manages positions, and exits when momentum fades — all without human intervention. Learn more in our Trading Strategies Guide.

Values

  • Transparency — We share our strategies openly, including what works and what doesn't.
  • Education First — We teach you how to trade, not just what to trade.
  • Risk Awareness — We emphasize stop-losses, position sizing, and momentum exits.
  • South African Focus — ZAR markets, Luno, SARS tax law — we cater specifically to SA investors.
